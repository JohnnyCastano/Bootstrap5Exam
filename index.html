My Top JDM Cars

The JDM cars, or Japanese Domestic Market cars, are some of the most popular cars in the world. These cars are only made for the Japanese market, although new laws now allow the export of cars 25 years and older for those that want that (this doesn't include the cars already here). These cars have revolutionized the car scene.

Toyota Supra

The Toyota Supra is my favorite JDM car of all. I was introduced to this car through the Fast and Furious movie series (as well as many others in this list). I love this car for the smooth surface as well as the rear and front of the car. I especially like the body-kits that you can use on the whole car, like the one in the first movie of the Fast and Furious movie.

  • Made in 1994
  • Original Price: $31,100 - $49,900
  • Price today: $23,000

Introduction

1958, that was the year that the first ever JDM (Japanese Domestic Market) cars were put on sale for the American's by Toyota!

The Kick-starter

The Datsun 510 is said to be the kick-starter for the JDM car tuning scene. First made in 1968. Also is the car made to be accepted by the American and Canadian Markets!

The First Ever

The first ever JDM car made in 1904, was called Yamaba's omnibus which was powered by steam and could carry 10 passengers!

Some of the World-Wide Favorite JDM Cars!

Seen by me and many others as some of the greatest JDM Cars for drifting, racing, and tuning!

The Mitsubishi Lancer Evo X was another car that I liked when watching Fast and Furious Tokyo Drift, this was a movie that introduced many if the JDM cars that we know today.This car is driven by the main character. I liked this one because of the compactness of the car as well as it being a 4 door.
The 1995 Honda Civic is one of my favorite cars not only because of the smooth design but because my dad owned this exact model. I have had a lot memories with this car and enjoyed being with it.
